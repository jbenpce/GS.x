Battery storage

~1.4 GW of battery energy storage

Our storage portfolio combines a ~960 MW wholly-owned pipeline with a further 400 MW developed through a strategic joint venture. We target 50–99 MW projects on 36 kV and 150 kV connections — the size and voltage where grid capacity, permitting and returns align best.

Solar PV

A proprietary solar pipeline

Alongside storage, we develop a proprietary photovoltaic pipeline in-house. Increasingly, we co-locate solar with batteries to create hybrid assets that capture more value from each grid connection and smooth renewable output.
