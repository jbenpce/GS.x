Why storage, why now

Intelligence runs on
electricity.

The intelligence economy — AI, data centres and electrified industry — is colliding with a grid built for a different age. Renewables are now the cheapest power ever built, but they are variable.

Grid-scale battery storage is the missing layer that turns abundant clean power into firm, dispatchable, always-on capacity. That makes it no longer a clean-energy nice-to-have, but critical infrastructure for the intelligence economy — the foundation everything intelligent is built on.

01

Soaring demand

AI, data centres and electrification are driving the steepest growth in power demand in a generation.

02

Variable supply

Solar and wind are the cheapest energy ever built — but they don't deliver on demand.

03

The firming layer

Batteries bridge the gap, delivering clean power exactly when and where the grid needs it.
