Our approach

Development
at its most
precise.

We originate, develop and structure energy storage assets from land to ready-to-build — combining deep local market knowledge with institutional-grade execution.

Starting in Italy, with the ambition to scale across Europe.

01

Project Origination

Proprietary site identification and land rights. We move before the market through local networks and technical screening.

02

Grid Connection & Permitting

Full management of STMG, Autorizzazione Unica and grid procedures — navigating Italy's complex regulatory environment.

03

Structuring & Capital

Equity partnerships, JV structures and co-development arrangements designed to align incentives across the project lifecycle.

04

Asset Monetisation

Ready-to-build project sales, PPA structuring and long-term ownership — multiple exit routes to maximise value.
