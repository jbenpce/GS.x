What we do

An integrated clean-energy developer

We combine deep local origination with disciplined development to bring storage and generation projects from greenfield to ready-to-build.

Battery storage (BESS)

Grid-scale battery systems that balance supply and demand, store surplus renewable power and deliver flexibility services to the grid.

Solar PV

A proprietary photovoltaic pipeline, developed in-house and increasingly paired with storage to maximise grid value and project economics.

Origination to RTB

We secure land and grid, manage permitting and engineering, and de-risk each project through to ready-to-build status.

Our strategy

Right-sized projects on the right grid nodes.

We focus on 50–99 MW projects connected at 36 kV and 150 kV — a sweet spot that balances grid availability, permitting speed and returns. Our first two batteries, totalling 130 MW, are in southern Italy with land and grid already secured.

  • 50–99 MW per project
  • 36 kV / 150 kV connections
  • Southern Italy focus
  • RTB target Q3 2027
